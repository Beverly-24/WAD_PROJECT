About us

FarmTracker is a smart digital platform built to connect farmers and buyers seamlessly. Our goal is to help farmers easily record and track their crop and livestock production, reduce waste, and reach trusted buyers directly. By simplifying farm management and transactions, we empower local farmers to grow their businesses and strengthen food supply chains with transparency and efficiency.s.
